Chocolate Chip Bannana Bread Recipe

  • Prep Time 15 minutes
  • Cook Time 50 minutes

Ingredients

List

  • 1 Stick(1/2 cup) Butter
  • 3 Large Very Ripe Bannanas
  • 2 LargeEggs
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • i teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoonCinnamon
  • 1 1/2 cupsChocolate Chips

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  1. Spay Loaf pan with non-stick spray or grease with butter
  2. Add Butter to large bowl and microwave for 1 minute or until melted
  3. Add Bannanas to butter and mash with fork until blended
  4. Add Vanilla Extract and Egg to bannanas and mix well
  5. In a second bowl Whisk together Flour,Sugar,Baking Soda, Baking Powder,Salt and Cinnamon
  6. Add dry ingredients to wet ingrediants and mix just until blended
  7. Add Chocolate Chips and mix just until blended but do not over mix
  8. Pour into prepared loaf pan
  9. Bake 45-55 minutes or until tooth pick comes out clean
